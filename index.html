Percentage

Percentage is a number that describes a part of the whole,
it is usually written as a number divided by 100.

For example 30 out of 100 is 30%

Usage in real life:

  • Proportion
    • Example:
      There are 10 people riding a motorcycle. 6 of them are wearing helmets, 4 of them are not.
      the number of people who are wearing helmets is 60%
  • Capacity
    • Example:
      There is a bottle with a 250ml capacity. The bottle is filled with 100ml of water.
      The bottle is 40% full.
  • Discounts
    • Example:
      There is a chair that costs Rp 50,000. It has a 20% discount.
      The total price of the chair is Rp 40,000.
  • Taxes
    • Example:
      A fried rice costs Rp 30,000. It has a 10% tax
      The total price of the fried rice is Rp 33,000.
  • etc.
